Passing of Passage

The breeze of wind had always accompanied me everyday, the smell of sea and the never-ending horizon guided the way. They came to me for a greeting, something that would always be there smiling. Like glittering of sand, soft and warmed by the sun, the emotions that last and will forever stand. The stars had spoken to me through the ocean, they asked me in return to write them stories and put them into motion. Tales that could reach even the deepest of the heart, words that would not be less than an art. Playing like a symphony, dancing as if a memory, to bring something that is happy. So, I decided to create these tales to tell you, one day you might have the same dream as I do. A shallow light upon the sea, hollow but colorful for the eyes to see, started to row my soul to where it should be.

Salty Path

25 December 2025

I have been working on an original fantasy novel for quite awhile now, however I have yet to find a publisher. The whole plot have yet to be finished, I wish to revise this so it would be very fun for people to enjoy.

The story takes place in a future world where the sea becomes a deadly place due to the changing nature of earth and the damage humanity has done. Half of the population in the sea was lost, it became a place not kind to men. After moving away to a place where the sky still lit the ocean while following her father's work. She was reminded of the beauty the sea once held. Near the shore laughing happily, a stupid looking young boy row the his tiny boat away to the deadly sea. A young sailor boy with dreams to meet the whale.
